## Account Management

### Account Information
<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Edit Account Information" icon="user-pen">
    Update your basic account details:
    - Username
    - Email address
    - Reset password
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Edit Profile" icon="id-card">
    Modify your public profile:
    - Profile photo
    - Name
    - Social links
    - Interests
    - Phone number
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Reset Password" icon="key">
    Change your account password:
    - We'll send an email with a link to reset your password
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Update Eventbrite Authentication" icon="link">
    Connect or update your Eventbrite account:
    - Link accounts for event importing
    - Manage permissions
    - Sync settings
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Manage Ticket Scanners" icon="qrcode">
    Control who can scan tickets at your events:
    - Manage scanners for all your events
    - Remove scanners
    - Get scanner invite codes
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Attendance Privacy" icon="user-shield">
    Control who can see your event attendance:
    - Public: Anyone can see
    - Private: Only you can see
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
