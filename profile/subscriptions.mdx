## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I change plans anytime?">
    Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What happens if I downgrade?">
    You'll maintain access to your current plan until the end of your billing cycle.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Are there any contracts?">
    No long-term contracts. Cancel or change plans anytime.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
