## Sending Mass Texts

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Recipients">
    Choose who receives the message:
    - Select individual recipients with checkboxes
    - Use header checkbox to select all
    - View selected count
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send Mass Text">
    Click the **Mass Text** button to compose your message.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Schedule Delivery">
    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="Send Now">
        Message will be sent immediately
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="Custom Schedule">
        Pick specific date and time
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="Event Day">
        Automatically send 12 hours before event
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="One Week Before">
        Automatically send 7 days before event start
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Custom Phone Number">
    Use a custom phone number to send messages from instead of PeerPop's default number.
    - Manage numbers in [Account Settings](/account/settings)
    - Better brand recognition
    - Improved delivery rates

    <Note>
      Requires [Essentials Plan](/account/subscriptions).
    </Note>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Compose Message">
    Customize your text:
    
    <AccordionGroup>
      <Accordion title="Personalization">
        - Include greeting (ex: "Hey, [recipient first name]")
        - Include signature (ex: "From, [username]")
        - Include event link
      </Accordion>
      
      <Accordion title="Message Content">
        - Enter message (160 chars per text)
        - Messages longer than 160 characters use multiple texts but will be sent as a single message to the recipient
        - Generate with AI assistant
      </Accordion>
    </AccordionGroup>
  </Step>
</Steps>
