<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Attending Events">
    <AccordionGroup>
      <Accordion title="How do PeerPop tickets work?">
        PeerPop tickets are digital and include a unique QR code for entry. You can:
        - Access tickets through your PeerPop account
        - Receive tickets via email and text
        - Show QR code at event for scanning
      </Accordion>

      <Accordion title="Is PeerPop available on all devices?">
        Yes! PeerPop is available on all devices via the web (Google Chrome, Safari, etc.)

        We also have a IOS app for ticket scanners.

        Be on the lookout for a full IOS app soon!
      </Accordion>

      <Accordion title="How do I get a refund?">
        Refund policies vary between event organizers who use PeerPop.

        Please contact the creator of the event on the event page in order to request a refund on your tickets.

        Contact us at support@peerpop.io if you have any questions or are unable to reach the event creator.
        
        <Note> Per our terms of service, all service fees are non-refundable. </Note>
      </Accordion>

      <Accordion title="Is my money secure on PeerPop?">
        All card numbers are encrypted at rest with AES-256. Decryption keys are stored on separate machines. PeerPop uses Stripe's infrastructure for storing, decrypting, and transmitting card numbers runs in a separate hosting environment, and doesn’t share any credentials with PeerPop's or Stripe's primary services including our API and website.
      </Accordion>
    </AccordionGroup>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Promoting Events">
    <AccordionGroup>
      <Accordion title="How do I become a promoter?">
        Getting started as a promoter is easy:
        1. Create a PeerPop account
        2. Find an event you want to promote
        3. Click **Start Earning** on the event page with a reward offer
        4. Get your unique tracking link
        5. Start sharing with your friends or on social media to start earning rewards
      </Accordion>

      <Accordion title="How do I find events to promote?">
        Discover promotion offers on PeerPop by:
        - Browsing events with reward tags and registering as a promoter
        - Filtering by reward types (commission, discounts, rewards)
        - Getting invited by event organizers
      </Accordion>

      <Accordion title="How does PeerPop help me promote?">
        PeerPop connects you with event organizers who are looking for promoters.

        You can earn rewards by sharing the event with your friends or on social media. 
      </Accordion>

      <Accordion title="How can I receive payouts for promoting on PeerPop?">
        This is only for commission based promotions. 

        Earning payouts is straightforward:
        1. Verify your financial information with Stripe.
        2. Connect your payout method (bank account/debit card)
        3. Earn commission through promotion
        4. Cash out through your wallet
        5. Choose standard (1-3 days) or instant transfer (deposit to your bank account)
        
        [Learn more about payouts](/finance/setup-payouts)
      </Accordion>
    </AccordionGroup>
  </Tab>

      <Accordion title="How do I sell tickets on PeerPop?">
        Selling tickets is simple:
        1. Verify your financial information with Stripe [Learn more](/finance/setup-payouts)
        2. Create a paid event on PeerPop
        3. Set up ticket types and pricing
        4. Configure sales dates
        5. Enable promotional tools
        6. Launch your event
        7. Track sales in real-time

      <Accordion title="How do I receive payouts from my events on PeerPop?">

        1. Complete Stripe verification
        2. Connect your bank account or debit card
        3. Choose standard (1-3 days) or instant transfer (3% fee) in your [Wallet](/finance/wallet)
        4. Track earnings in real-time
        
        [Set up your payouts](/finance/setup-payouts)
      </Accordion>
