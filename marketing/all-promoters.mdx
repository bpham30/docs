## Promoter List

View all promoters across your events. Each entry shows:
- Promoter name
- Total tickets sold
- Total revenue generated
- Total link clicks
- Events promoted

<Tip>
  Click the dropdown arrow to view contact information, events promoted, and copy their unique tracking links.
</Tip>

## Search & Sort

Find specific promoters quickly:
- Search by name
- Filter by event
- Sort columns by clicking headers:
  - Tickets sold
  - Revenue generated
  - Link clicks
  - Join date

## Mass Communication

Send messages to selected promoters:
- Select promoters using checkboxes
- Choose "Select All" for entire list
- Send [Mass Text](/manage-events/mass-texts) or [Mass Email](/manage-events/mass-emails)
