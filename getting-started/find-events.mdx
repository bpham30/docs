## Search & Filter Events

PeerPop makes it easy to find events that match your interests.

### Search Options

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Location Based" icon="location-dot">
    Find events in your city or search other locations
  </Card>
  <Card title="Category Filter" icon="filter">
    Filter by music, sports, arts, networking, and more
  </Card>
  <Card title="Date Range" icon="calendar">
    Search by date or browse upcoming events
  </Card>
  <Card title="Price Range" icon="tag">
    Filter events by ticket price or find free events
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Exploring Other Cities

<Steps>
  <Step title="Change Location">
    Click the location selector in the top navigation
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select City">
    Choose from popular cities or search for any location
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse Events">
    View all events in your selected city
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Enable location services to automatically see events near you and get personalized recommendations.
</Tip>
