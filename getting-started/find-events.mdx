## Browse Events

Find events that match your interests or earn rewards by promoting events in your area. You can:
- Browse events by city
- Filter by category, date, and reward offers
- Earn rewards by promoting events you love

<Note>
  Events with reward offers are marked with a green tag showing available rewards, discounts, or commission.
</Note>

## Finding Events

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Your City">
    Choose your location to see nearby events:
    - Browse featured cities
    - Find events near me
  </Step>

  <Step title="Filter Events">
    Narrow down events using:
    - **Categories**: Music, Comedy, Nightlife, etc.
    - **Date**: Today, This Weekend, Custom Range
    - **Reward Offers**: Show events with promoter rewards
  </Step>

  <Step title="Explore Event Details">
    Each event card shows:
    - Event flyer and title
    - Date and time
    - Location
    - Available rewards (if any)
    
    Click any event to see full details, buy tickets, or start promoting
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Promoter Rewards

Promote events and earn rewards by sharing your unique tracking link with friends and followers. 

<Note>
  Events with reward offers are marked with a green tag showing available rewards, discounts, or commission.
</Note>

### How It Works
1. Choose events you want to promote and sign up as a promoter on the **Event Page**
2. Get your **unique tracking link** and share it with friends and followers
3. Track progress and collect rewards when goals are met in the **Rewards Dashboard**
4. Cash out your earnings in the **Wallet** (only for commissions)
