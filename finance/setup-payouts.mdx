## Getting Started

Before you can receive payouts from ticket sales or promotional commissions, you'll need to verify your business information through Stripe, our payment processor.

<Note>
  This is a one-time setup process. Once verified, you can receive payouts for all future events and promotions.
</Note>

## Setup Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Wallet">
    1. Navigate to your Wallet
    2. Click "Update Business Information" button
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Stripe Verification">
    You'll be redirected to Stripe's secure platform to provide:
    
    <AccordionGroup>
      <Accordion title="Financial Details" icon="credit-card">
        - Bank account information
        - Routing number
        - Account number
        - Account type
      </Accordion>
      
      <Accordion title="Business Information" icon="building">
        - Business name
        - Business type
        - Tax ID/EIN
        - Business address
      </Accordion>
      
      <Accordion title="Personal Information" icon="user">
        - Legal name
        - Date of birth
        - SSN (last 4 digits)
        - Home address
      </Accordion>
    </AccordionGroup>

    <Note>
      Peerpop does not have access, store, or use your financial information. It's securely processed by Stripe.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Connect Payout Method">
    Choose your preferred payout method:
    - Bank account
    - Debit card
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Verification Complete">
    After successful verification:
    - You'll be redirected back to PeerPop
    - Your wallet will be activated
    - You can start receiving payouts
  </Step>
</Steps>

## After Verification

Once verified, you can:
- Create paid events
- Receive ticket sale revenue
- Earn promotional commissions
- Track earnings in your wallet

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Paid Events" icon="ticket">
    Sell tickets and receive revenue directly to your connected account
  </Card>
  <Card title="Promoter Earnings" icon="money-bill">
    Earn commission for promoting other events
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Warning>
  Keep your business and banking information up to date to avoid any delays in receiving payouts.
</Warning>
