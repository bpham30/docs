## Wallet Overview

Access your wallet from the sidebar to view:
- Available balance
- Pending funds
- Transaction history

<Note>
  Pending funds are payments still in processing and have not yet settled into your wallet.
</Note>

## Transaction List

View all financial activity:
- Date
- Time
- Type
  - Payout
  - Payment
  - Refund
- Amount
